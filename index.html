About This Game

The Adventures of Lewis & Clark is an interactive educational game that puts you in the boots of the Corps of Discovery on their historic 1804–1806 expedition across the American West.

Make real decisions about what to pack, who to trust, and how to navigate — just as Meriwether Lewis and William Clark did more than 200 years ago.

Learning Objectives: U.S. westward expansion, geography, Native American diplomacy, and frontier survival.

Recommended for grades 4 – 8.
