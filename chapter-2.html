Up the Missouri River

Guide the boat upstream through the Missouri River. Dodge floating logs and rocks to reach your destination safely.

How to Play

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to steer the boat.
  • Avoid rocks and logs as they move down the river toward you.
  • Each round is different — obstacles appear randomly.
  • Reach the end of the river to complete the minigame.
